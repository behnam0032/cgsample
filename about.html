About CGSample

CGSample develops plugins and tools that make working with Autodesk 3ds Max easier and more efficient for architects and architectural visualization professionals.

The focus is on simplifying real-world architectural workflows inside 3ds Max through practical, purpose-built plugins. CGSample tools are designed to reduce repetitive tasks, improve control over complex scenes, and streamline everyday production processes.

Rather than adding unnecessary complexity, CGSample plugins aim to remove friction from common architectural workflows by offering clear, targeted solutions that integrate naturally into existing pipelines.

Core Principles

  • Workflow simplification for architectural projects
  • Automation of repetitive technical tasks
  • Better scene organization and control
  • Performance-aware solutions for heavy 3ds Max scenes

Each plugin is developed with a clear goal: to make professional architectural work in 3ds Max more manageable, predictable, and efficient.

CGSample is built for users who value practical tools, clean workflows, and solutions designed specifically for architectural production.
